About This Property

This stunning modern apartment is located in the heart of the 5th Settlement in New Cairo, offering a premium living experience with high-end finishes throughout. The apartment features an open-plan living and dining area that flows onto a spacious balcony with panoramic city views.

The kitchen is fully fitted with imported cabinets and granite countertops. All three bedrooms feature built-in wardrobes, with the master bedroom having an en-suite bathroom. The property is in a secured compound with 24/7 security, a swimming pool, and a fully equipped gym.

The apartment is situated on the 7th floor of a 12-floor building, ensuring excellent natural light and privacy. It is within walking distance of major malls, international schools, and medical centers. The compound offers underground parking, a children's playground, and beautifully landscaped gardens.
