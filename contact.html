Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Is listing a property on AQARX really free?
Yes — completely free. Any registered user can list their property with photos, details, and pricing at no cost. No hidden fees, no commissions, no contracts.
How does the AI Interior Visualizer work?
Upload a photo of your empty room, select a style (Modern, Classic, Oriental, etc.), and our FLUX AI generates a furnished version of your space. It's powered by Stable Diffusion and Llama AI through our Cloudflare infrastructure.
How do I become a verified developer on AQARX?
Register a developer account and submit your company documents. Our team verifies within 2-3 business days. Once verified, you get the blue badge and access to developer-specific features.
Can decoration companies sell furnished apartments on AQARX?
Yes! Decoration companies can register as partners and list fully furnished apartments for sale, offer their design services, and connect directly with buyers who used the AI visualizer.
What areas does AQARX cover?
We currently cover 27 cities across Egypt including all of Greater Cairo, Alexandria, North Coast, Red Sea, Ain Sokhna, and more. We're expanding monthly.
